Interactive 3D animation

Enhancing Customer Experience through Interactive 3D Product Animation.

Project Overview

This project involved developing an interactive 3D animation for a web store. The animation was designed to enhance customer understanding of the product by depicting its features and functions from various angles. The aim was to provide a user-friendly and engaging visual aid to help customers make informed purchasing decisions.

My Role

As the lead 3D animator, I directed the full animation process, from inception to final rendering. Collaborating with a software engineer, I ensured our interactive 3D animation functioned seamlessly on the website, incorporating senior feedback throughout the project.
